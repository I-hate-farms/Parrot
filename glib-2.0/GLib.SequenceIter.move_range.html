move_range

Description:

public void move_range (SequenceIter<G> begin, SequenceIter<G> end)

Inserts the (begin, end) range at the destination pointed to by ptr.

The begin and end iters must point into the same sequence. It is allowed for dest to point to a different sequence than the one pointed into by begin and end.

If dest is NULL, the range indicated by begin and end is removed from the sequence. If dest iter points to a place within the (begin, end) range, the range does not move.

begin

a SequenceIter
end

a SequenceIter
