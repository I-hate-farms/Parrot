IOStreamSpliceFlags

Description:

[ Flags ]
public enum IOStreamSpliceFlags

GIOStreamSpliceFlags determine how streams should be spliced.


Namespace: GLib
Package: gio-2.0

Content:

Enum values:

  • NONE - Do not close either stream.
  • CLOSE_STREAM1 - Close the first stream after the splice.
  • CLOSE_STREAM2 - Close the second stream after the splice.
  • WAIT_FOR_BOTH - Wait for both splice operations to finish before calling the callback.
